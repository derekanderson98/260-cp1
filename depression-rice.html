Depression Rice

This recipe is... not very good. The result of a stressful finals week where I, still single, lost all control of myself in the attempt to get my stomach to shut up so I could focus on studying when everything seemed to taste the same. I needed a new flavor, so I made one with what I had. I look back at this and laugh at the things I did when I was single, so I included it here as a memory. Serves one while they dejectedly watch cartoons on the couch, head spinning from exam stress.

You will need:

  • 1/2 cup white rice
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • parsley flakes
  • paprika
  • salt
  • rice cooker
  1. Cook the rice in the rice cooker
  2. Add the garlic and onion powder, parsley and paprika. However much you think it needs. Use three times the paprika than the amount you used of anything else.
  3. Put a lot of salt on it. The dish will taste very bitter without it.
  4. Stir and eat.
  5. Question your life choices.
